I am a second-year M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2020. My research interests include Robotics and Computer Vision.
